Da Lat surprised me. The cool mountain air after weeks in the heat of the south felt surreal — like stepping into a different country entirely.

The lake at the center of town is the perfect place to slow down. Rent a swan pedal boat, grab a cup of cà phê trứng from one of the stalls nearby, and just exist for a while.
