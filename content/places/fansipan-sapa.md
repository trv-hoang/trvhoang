Standing at 3,143 meters, Fansipan is the highest peak in Indochina. On a clear day you can see forever — layered mountain ridges fading into the horizon, terraced rice fields far below, and absolute silence except for the wind.

The cable car makes it accessible, but the view is no less earned. Bring a jacket — it's cold up there even in summer.
