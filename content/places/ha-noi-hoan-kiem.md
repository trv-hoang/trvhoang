Where it all started. Walking around Hoan Kiem Lake at dawn, watching the mist rise off the water while locals practice tai chi — there's no better introduction to Hanoi.

The Ngoc Son Temple on the small island in the middle of the lake is worth the short walk across the red Huc Bridge. Everything about this place feels like the soul of the city.
